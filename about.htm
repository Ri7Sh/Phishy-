About Us

E-commerce venture for fresh, chemical-free seafood. Within 24 hours from the sea to ensure you get the most fresh seafood possible We give maximum value to the fishermen and consumer by cutting off the middle-men We assure 100% chemical free fish and have stringent lab testing done to verify samples of fish Some of our premium fish is “hook-caught” or caught the traditional way using a line and a hook and thus will be much superior in taste than the mass produced trawler caught fish A venture by seafood industry veterans who have been dealing with fish for decades Powered by a dynamic website, with backend linked live to our processing plants Products delivered within hours of the catch landing in our plants We only use natural ice for packing and delivery Delivery at customer’s doorstep by own specialized logistics team Cash on delivery and psy-online. Our magic mantra: Shore to door. To ensure the process is quick and convenient, PHISHY has a vast home-delivery network and branded retail presence. You can now step into any of our retail outlet partners to enjoy high quality seafood.
